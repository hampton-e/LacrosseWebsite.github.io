About Lacrosse


Lacrosse is one of the fastest growing sports in the world! Theres no better time to play it then now!

Lacrosse is a team sport which is played with a lacrosse stick and a lacrosse ball. It is popular sport worldwide but mostly known for being played in America.


This website aims to encourage young New Zealand high school students, like you, to play Lacrosse.

RULES

There are 10 players on the field
- Three attackers
- Three defenders
- Three Mid Fielders
- One Goalie
Equipment
- Goggles (eye protection)
- Lacrosse stick
- Lacrosse ball
- Mouth guard
- and Cleats are recommended

Why You Should Play

There are so many great reasons as to why you should play Lacrosse as a sport!

  • Improve sportsmanship and teamwork: Lacrosse is a fantastic sport which will allow you to communicate with your teammates. You will be able to motivate one another so that you will all be able to play to the best of your abilities.
  • Make friends: Playing a team sport will give you the opportunity to create lifelong friendships. It is such a great way to be able to connect with others.
  • Develop personal skills: Playing a sport will help you to develop skills such as time management. Playing a sport will help you become organised as you were learn to manage your time with sport and school work.
  • Increse confidence: Playing a sport helps you to build confidence on and off the field - boosting self esteem and self confidence.
  • Fun: It is a great way to stay busy and keep your mind and body active. You will also find people with the same interests as you while you are at it!
  • Natural stress reliever: This is a good way to take a break from a long day of school work and also a good way to loosen up. It's a great way to get fresh air into your lungs!
  • Physical health: You will be able to keep fit in a fun and exciting way!

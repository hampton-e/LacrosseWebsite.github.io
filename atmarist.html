Lacrosse At Marist

Lacrosse is one of the many sports which are offered at Marist College.

Here are some basics skils to help you prepare for trainings. To the right are some useful videos which will you an understanding of the game.

Lacrosse is a winter sport which are played in term 2 and 3 of the school year. The games at held every thursdays, each week of the term.

Each week the venue will be different depending on which team you will be playing against. These are usually held at the schools ground on the field.

There are also trainings which will be held once or twice a week on the school field at 3:30pm. This will usually run til 5pm.
